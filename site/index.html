Interlude

Interlude started as a hobby project, made for fun in my spare time.
My aim is to create a client packed with all the cool ideas the community and I come up with towards providing my "ideal VSRG experience"

Download Interlude

User interface

I'm not a UI designer by trade (and I've never made a website from scratch before either!)
I'm proud to say I've trial-and-errored my way into a design language that suits me, and I hope you find yourself comfortable navigating everything.

Ingame help

Don't know what a button does? Forgotten how something works?
I know I have. That's why I've built helpful tooltips into the game. Just press the / key while hovering over most things to see an explanation of what it is and how to use it

Features

Everything you would normally expect to be a setting in a rhythm game, Interlude has it.
This includes custom skins for your notes, and more advanced features like lane covers or pacemakers

Customise your HUD

You can reposition the gameplay HUD elements however you like, and also change a variety of settings about what they display.
