Interlude

Interlude is a hobby project I originally started working on for fun in 2018.
In 2021 I finished university, so I've gotten back into steadily working on the project.
The aim is to make a VSRG client the way I like it, packed with cool ideas to provide my "ideal VSRG experience" - If you like the same things as me, you will surely like this client too.

Download Interlude

Features

Everything you would normally expect to be a setting in a rhythm game, Interlude has it.
This includes custom skins for your notes, and more advanced features like lane covers or pacemakers.

User interface

I'm no designer, and yet I'm proud to say I've trial-and-errored my way into a design language that suits me.
You should find yourself quite comfortable, but if you have suggestions for improvements, please share them in the discord!

Ingame help

Don't know what a button does? Forgotten how something works?
I know I have. That's why I've built helpful tooltips into the game. Just press the / key while hovering over most things to see an explanation of what it is and how to use it

Customise your HUD

You can reposition the gameplay HUD elements however you like, and also change a variety of settings about what they display.

Like what you see?

Interlude can only grow to its full potential with your help.
The best way to help out is to join the community and provide feature requests/playtesting feedback.
